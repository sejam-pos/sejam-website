Frequently Asked Questions

Can I purchase the system now ?
We haven't launched yet. But you can go ahead and fill the “request a demo” form to give us your contact details and join our waitlist for a free one month trial :).
Can I purchase the POS hardware from your website too ?
Yes, you can choose either a software only purchase plan or if you need, also purchase the hardware from our website. We provide tablets, barcode scanners, cash registers and tablet stands. Contact us directly and we can give you more detail.
How much does the system cost ?
We want to have an affordable system that will make you happy. Our pricing plan will be released at launch. A subscription fee will cover the costs of the software.
What about other retail, can I still use your POS ?
Yes, of course. Our user interface is simple, and if you have a small shop our features are versatile enough to fit many of your needs.
